Tips for getting the most value

Be Honest

This is your private space—nobody sees it. The more honest you are, the better your insights. Don't game the system—you only hurt yourself.

Make It a Habit

Schedule 15 minutes every Friday. Reflect on the week's leadership moments. Consistency compounds over time.

Focus on One Thing

Don't try to fix everything at once. Pick one skill per month. Practice, reflect, adjust. Move to next skill when ready.

Apply Immediately

Don't just read—do. Try one new behavior each week. Notice what happens. Adjust based on results.
